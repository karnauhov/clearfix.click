Information We Collect

When you use our website or mobile application (the "Service"), we may collect the following types of personal information from you:

  • Personal Details: name, email address, phone number, postal address, company name (if applicable).
  • Account Information: username, password, and other registration information you provide when creating an account.
  • Service Details: information about the jobs or services requested or provided through our platform (including job descriptions, addresses, photos, etc.), and any ratings or feedback you provide.
  • Communications: any messages, comments, or other information you submit through the Service or send to our customer support.
  • Device and Usage Data: information collected automatically when you use the Service, such as your IP address, device type, operating system, browser information, and your interactions with the website and app.
  • Analytics Data: data collected through Google Analytics about how you use our Service, including pages visited, time spent on pages, and other usage statistics.
  • Location Data: approximate or precise location information from your device if you permit location services (for example, to connect you with professionals in your area).

We do not collect any payment card details. All payments are processed through third-party payment providers. We do not store your credit or debit card information on our systems.

How We Use Your Information

We use the personal information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and manage the Service: to facilitate the provision of services, to communicate with you about your account and orders, and to respond to your requests or inquiries.
  • To process payments: to complete transactions through third-party payment processors. ClearFix does not handle or store your payment card data.
  • To improve and develop our services: to analyze usage of the Service, to enhance and customize the user experience, to fix issues, and to improve our platform.
  • To send promotional communications: with your consent, we may send newsletters, offers, or other marketing materials. You can withdraw your consent at any time.
  • For customer support: to respond to your questions, comments, or complaints, and to provide support.
  • To comply with legal obligations: to detect and prevent fraud, enforce our terms, and comply with applicable laws and regulations.

How We Share Your Information

We may share your personal information with:

  • Service Professionals: the professionals you hire or engage through our platform. For example, if you request a service, we will share your name, contact details, and job information with the selected professional so they can perform the work.
  • Service Providers and Partners: such as payment processors, hosting providers, email service providers, analytics providers (like Google Analytics), and other third-party vendors who assist us in operating our Service. These third parties are contractually obligated to keep your information confidential.

  • Aggregated or De-identified Data: we may use or share aggregated demographic or usage data that does not directly identify you.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website and app to improve user experience and analyze usage. These may include:

  • Cookies: small data files stored on your device to recognize your browser and remember your preferences.
  • Google Analytics: we use Google Analytics to collect information about use of our Service. Google Analytics collects data such as your IP address (which may be anonymized), browser type, device information, and pages visited. For more information on how Google Analytics collects and processes data, see the Google Privacy Policy.
